PRIDE by the Beach

The North County LGBTQ Resource Center's annual pride event, gathering people from all along San Diego County, is making a comeback this year. Happening on June 11th, 2022, PBTB is a massive celebration of pride featuring many vendors and performs all coming together to talk about pride and advocate for a better world. Some of the events this year are Paris Sukomi Max, a drag queen performing different disney classics. A lot of different food vendors are going to be there as well.

This event has been going on for 15 years, and is only growing in it's size and impact. It's a public and free event that anyone and everyone are allowed to come to, and promises to be amazing this year.
